Why SadaqaWorks?

Inspired by the Prophet Muhammad's (peace be upon him) approach to empowering others through work and dignity, our mission is to transform charity into self-sufficiency.

"A man from the Ansaar came to the Prophet and begged from him... (Full Hadith below)"
Read Full Hadith

A man from the Ansaar came to the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) and asked him for charity. The Prophet said: “Have you nothing in your house?” The man said, “Yes—only a piece of cloth: we wear part of it and spread part of it; and a wooden bowl from which we drink water.” The Prophet said, “Bring them to me.” So the man brought them, and the Prophet took them in his hand and asked, “Who will buy these?” A man said, “I will buy them for one dirham.” The Prophet said, “Who will offer more than one dirham?” Another man said, “I will buy them for two dirhams.” He sold them for two dirhams, gave the money to the Ansari, and said: “Buy food with one dirham and take it to your family. And buy an axe-head with the other and bring it to me.” The man brought the axe-head, and the Prophet fixed a handle to it with his own hand and said: “Go and gather firewood and sell it. Do not let me see you for fifteen days.” The man went and did so, then returned having earned ten dirhams. He bought clothing and food. The Prophet (peace be upon him) said: “This is better for you than begging, for begging will come as a mark on a person’s face on the Day of Resurrection.”

Inspiration Behind the Platform

Our approach is rooted in prophetic wisdom that highlights empowerment over dependency.

“This is better for you than begging, for begging will come as a mark on a person’s face on the Day of Resurrection.”

This Hadith reminds us that work, no matter how simple, brings dignity and reward. SadaqaWorks was created to bring this same opportunity to every hand once stretched out in need.
